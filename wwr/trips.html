Our rafting experiences

Level one: Float

The level one is called "Float", it divides in four sub levels but the difficulty barely rises, this level means that you will have a guide who mostly will be paddling and you can relax, enjoy the view and be in calm waters and enjoy the moment!

Level two: Intermediate white water

These trips offer a little more adrenaline and an introduction to a true whitewater experience, There will be no big drops on this trip, Although some paddling is needed, you’ll still be able to enjoy the beauty of the river and unique moments these trip offer.

Level three: Advanced white water

Advanced white water rafting is designed for those wanting to fully immerse themselves, If you’re looking for an adrenaline anf fun experience you shoul try this trip!, you will raft in the waves, and work with your guide to avoid obstacles.
